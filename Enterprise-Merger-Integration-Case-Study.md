# Enterprise Merger Integration Case Study

## Project Overview

This portfolio example illustrates a large-scale commercial banking account conversion following a financial institution merger. It demonstrates the planning, analysis, coordination, and execution activities required to successfully migrate commercial customer accounts while minimizing operational risk and customer disruption.

> **Portfolio Note:** This case study is an original example inspired by enterprise banking merger activities. It does not contain confidential, proprietary, or employer-owned information.

---

## Business Challenge

Following the merger of two large financial institutions, thousands of commercial business accounts required migration into a unified banking platform.

Primary objectives included:

* Maintaining uninterrupted customer access
* Preserving account accuracy
* Meeting regulatory requirements
* Reducing operational risk
* Coordinating multiple business and technology teams

---

## Project Objectives

* Standardize commercial account data
* Validate customer information before conversion
* Reduce manual processing
* Improve conversion accuracy
* Minimize customer impact during migration
* Support successful post-conversion operations
