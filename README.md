# Business Analyst Portfolio

This repository contains original sample artifacts that demonstrate my approach to business analysis, requirements gathering, process improvement, and solution design.

The examples are representative of the work performed in enterprise financial services and technology organizations but do not contain confidential or proprietary information.

## Contents

### Business Requirements Documents (BRDs)

Sample documents that define business objectives, scope, requirements, assumptions, risks, and success criteria.

### Functional Requirements

Examples of translating business needs into functional requirements for technology teams.

### User Stories

Sample Agile user stories with acceptance criteria.

### Process Flow Diagrams

Business process documentation illustrating current-state and future-state workflows.

### Use Cases

Examples of end-to-end business scenarios with actors, triggers, preconditions, and expected outcomes.

### Requirements Traceability

Illustrative traceability matrices showing how requirements map to design, testing, and implementation.
