# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Project

Business Account Opening Workflow Enhancement

## Business Need

The existing business account opening process requires customers to submit duplicate information across multiple systems, resulting in increased processing time, manual effort, and a poor customer experience.

The objective of this project is to streamline the onboarding process by reducing duplicate data entry, improving workflow automation, and increasing visibility into application status.

---

## Business Objectives

* Reduce account opening time by 30%.
* Eliminate duplicate customer data entry.
* Improve data accuracy.
* Increase customer satisfaction.
* Reduce manual review workload.
* Improve regulatory compliance through standardized data collection.

## Functional Requirements

1. The system shall allow customers to save and resume an application.
2. The system shall validate required fields before submission.
3. The system shall notify applicants when documentation is missing.
4. The system shall provide real-time application status.
5. The system shall capture an audit trail of all workflow activities.
6. The system shall route applications based on business rules.

---

## Non-Functional Requirements

* 99.9% system availability
* Response time under two seconds
* Secure encryption of customer information
* Compliance with regulatory and internal security standards
