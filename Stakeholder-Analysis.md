# Stakeholder Analysis

## Executive Overview

Effective stakeholder management is essential to the success of enterprise technology initiatives. This stakeholder analysis identifies key stakeholder groups, their roles, business interests, influence, communication needs, and expected project contributions.

> **Portfolio Note:** This document is an original example created for professional portfolio purposes. It demonstrates stakeholder analysis techniques commonly used in enterprise business analysis and project delivery.

---

# Purpose

The purpose of this stakeholder analysis is to:

* Identify project stakeholders.
* Understand stakeholder expectations.
* Define communication strategies.
* Support effective decision-making.
* Promote stakeholder engagement throughout the project lifecycle.

# Stakeholder Responsibilities

## Executive Sponsor

* Provides strategic direction.
* Approves major project decisions.
* Removes organizational roadblocks.

## Business Owner

* Defines business priorities.
* Approves business requirements.
* Accepts final solution.

## Business Analyst

* Facilitates requirements gathering.
* Documents business processes.
* Coordinates stakeholder feedback.
* Supports solution validation.

## Technology Team

* Designs and implements technical solutions.
* Supports testing and deployment.
